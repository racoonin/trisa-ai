How to Use Voice Therapy

Getting Started

1. Click the microphone button to start recording

2. Speak naturally about what's on your mind

3. Click again to stop and receive a response

Voice Options

Toggle between male and female therapeutic voices using the switch above the microphone.

Safety & Privacy

Your conversations are processed securely and not permanently stored. If you're in crisis, please contact emergency services or a crisis helpline immediately.

Crisis Resources

US: 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)

UK: 116 123 (Samaritans)

Emergency: 911 (US), 999 (UK), 112 (EU)
